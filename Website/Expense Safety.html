Expense Management Tips

Budget Allocation

When managing your expenses, allocate 50% for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for debts/savings. Needs include groceries, transportation, utilities, rent/mortgage, and insurance. Ensure your expenses align with these categories.

Unnecessary Expenses

Review and eliminate expenses you no longer need. For example, if you're not using a service or membership, consider canceling it. Research alternatives to save money, as new options often offer better value.

Expense Tracking

Use our website to review your expenses. Sometimes people forget about recurring charges. If your output doesn't align with your statements, there might be something missing. Statements are a good place to check for forgotten expenses. Furthermore, if a category is bigger than expected, that is a good indicator. Productivity is yellow, health is green, and entertainment is blue.
